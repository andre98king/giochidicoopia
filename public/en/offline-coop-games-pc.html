Offline Co-op Games for PC

Play co-op without an internet connection: the best offline and local co-op titles for PC.

Not all co-op games require internet. A large category of titles supports local play — two or more players on the same PC or via local network — without needing a connection, online account or subscription.

This list collects the best offline co-op games for PC in 2026: titles with split screen, shared screen or LAN support, selected for local gameplay quality and Steam ratings. They're perfect for live evenings, LAN parties, or simply playing when internet isn't available or stable.

From co-op platformers to action games, from puzzles to RPGs, offline co-op covers every genre. Each card shows the maximum number of locally supported players and whether the game supports split screen or separate controllers.
