Fishing Planet® is a free-to-play and highly realistic first-person online multiplayer fishing simulator. Developed by avid fishing enthusiasts to bring you the full thrill of actual angling on your PC.

Fishing Planet supports online co-op for 1-4 players. Team up online for a shared adventure. Cooperative mechanics are central to the experience, making each session unique.

Categorized as sport, strategy, action, Fishing Planet offers a blend of gameplay styles for co-op enthusiasts. Released in 2017, it has been very well received by players and critics alike.
