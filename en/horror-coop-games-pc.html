Best Co-op Horror Games for PC

Survive together: the best co-op horror games for PC, from Left 4 Dead 2 to Phasmophobia.

Co-op horror has a special magic: shared fear amplifies everything. Every suspicious noise becomes a moment to discuss, every sudden death a collective shock, every survival a victory to celebrate.

This list features the best co-op horror games for PC in 2026, selected for co-op experience quality and real Steam ratings. From timeless classics like Left 4 Dead 2 — still unmatched after all these years — to recent phenomena like Phasmophobia, Lethal Company and R.E.P.O., which redefined the genre with investigation and asymmetric survival mechanics.

Whether you want to face zombie hordes, hunt ghosts or survive alien creatures, PC co-op horror offers experiences simply not possible to have alone.
